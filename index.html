Welcome to Blockadillo!


Objective:

Your goal is to make a tower to reach the top of the play area. To do this, stop the block when it reaches the center of the screen, the higher you go the more points you get! If you stop the block when it's not in the center, you lose and the game restarts.


Controls:

  • To start the game, press "Start"
  • To Pause the game at any time, press "Pause"
  • To stop the block from moving, press "Stop"
